## Description

'Risk Realm' is a strategy-based simulation game where teams compete to maximize the value of a shared asset portfolio over a five-year period by making smart risk-protection decisions under uncertainty.

The player begins with an insurance budget equal to 5% of the total asset value. Using this limited premium, they must decide how to allocate risk protection across a diverse set of assets located in different geographies and exposed to multiple natural catastrophe risks.

Each asset has a defined useful life and depreciates linearly over time, reducing its value year after year. Associated with every asset is a range of potential perils. While the occurrence of these events in any given year is uncertain, teams are provided with historical data and catastrophe models representing 100 years of event patterns to inform their decisions.

At the start of each year (round), events are randomly triggered based on the probability distributions.

If an asset is impacted by an event and is adequately insured, its value is preserved.

If an asset is impacted by an event without coverage, it is permanently lost and removed from the portfolio for all future years.

The player must revisit their insurance strategy every year, taking into account:

-> Asset depreciation

-> Changing asset values

-> Evolving risk exposure

-> Limited and fixed premium budgets

The objective is to maximize the cumulative Net Asset Value over five years.
The player with the highest total asset value at the end of the final year wins.

Success requires balancing risk, cost, and uncertainty—making informed trade-offs rather than attempting to insure everything.
